Policies

Enrolment, Payment & Refund Policy

Trial Class Policy

Every new student receives one free 30-minute trial session. No payment, no credit card, no obligation. The trial class gives you a chance to meet your teacher, ask questions, and decide if the style and pace suit you. Bookings are confirmed by email reply — no separate account needed.

Payment Terms

All courses are billed monthly in advance. Payment is accepted via UPI, bank transfer, or credit/debit card. Annual plans can be paid in one transaction and receive a 15% discount. No mid-month joining: if you join after the 15th, your billing starts from the 1st of the following month and we offer 2 complimentary sessions in the interim.

Rescheduling & Attendance

You may reschedule a session up to 24 hours before it begins, subject to teacher availability. Up to 2 reschedules per month are free. A third reschedule in the same month incurs a ₹200 administrative fee. Sessions missed without notice within 12 hours are forfeited. We do not carry forward unused sessions to the next month.

Refund Policy

Monthly plans: No refunds once the billing month has begun. If a student wishes to stop, they may do so at any time and no further charges will be made. Annual plans: A pro-rata refund is available within the first 30 days of purchase, provided no more than 2 sessions have been used. After 30 days or after 3+ sessions, annual plans are non-refundable. In the unlikely event that the Academy is unable to provide a scheduled session, that session will be rescheduled at the student's convenience or credited to the next billing period.

Level Assessment

Students who have some prior training and are unsure which level to join are welcome to book a free 30-minute assessment session. Based on the assessment, we will recommend the appropriate level and plan. Students who join at an incorrect level may switch to a higher or lower plan within the first month at no extra charge.
