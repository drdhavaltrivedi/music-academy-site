From Zero to Performance

The Path Every Student Takes

Understand Sound

Learn why some sounds feel pleasant and others don't — the physics behind music.

Learn the 7 Swaras

Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Dha Ni — memorise them, feel them, sing them in tune.

Master Alankars

Daily scale exercises that build speed, clarity, and muscle memory.

Learn a Taal

Count 16 beats, find the Sam, clap the vibhag — Teen Taal is your first rhythm.

Understand a Raag

Start with Yaman — learn its rules, its mood, and sing its Aaroh-Avaroh.

Sing a Bandish

Your first composition — a short song set inside a raag and taal.

Perform

Baithak, examination, or just for yourself — you now speak the language of raga.

Who Is This For?

Anyone. At Any Age. Any Background.
