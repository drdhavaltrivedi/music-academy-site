The Seven Swaras — सात स्वर

Saषड्ज
Reऋषभ
Gaगान्धार
Maमध्यम
Paपञ्चम
Dhaधैवत
Niनिषाद

Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Dha Ni — the foundation of all Hindustani music. Each swar carries its own emotional quality (rasa) and connects to the cosmos.

Sa and Pa are achala (fixed). The remaining 5 swaras each have a Shuddha, Komal, or Tivra form — creating the 12-tone scale.

Curriculum

Five Levels of Mastery

Our curriculum follows the traditional examination board syllabus, taking students from absolute beginners to accomplished performers.

1

Prarambhik

6–12 months

Introduction to sur, taal, and the 7 swaras. Basic alankars, simple bandishes, and Bhairavi thaat. Ideal for complete beginners.

2

Praveshika

1–1.5 years

Deeper swar sadhana, introduction to raags Yaman and Bhupali, taal Teen Taal (16 beats). Basic alaap and composition.

3

Madhyama

2 years

Study of 8–10 raags, khayal gayaki, thumri introduction. Detailed gamak and meend practice. Board examination preparation.

4

Visharad

2–3 years

Advanced raag grammar, bandish composition, raga identification, taal theory. Equivalent to a diploma in classical music.

5

Alankar

3+ years

The pinnacle — mastery of 40+ raags, elaborate khayal, taan, sargam, layakari. Suitable for professional performance and teaching.

What You Learn

A Complete Musical Education

Our holistic curriculum covers every dimension of Hindustani classical music — from the physics of sound to stage performance.

🎵

Swar Gyan (Tonal Knowledge)

  • 12 swaras and their shrutis
  • Shuddha, Komal & Tivra swaras
  • Swar sadhana and sur placement
  • Meend, Gamak, Khatka, Murki
  • Swar identification (ear training)
🥁

Taal & Laya (Rhythm)

  • Teen Taal (16 beats)
  • Ek Taal, Jhap Taal, Rupak
  • Dadra, Keherwa (light music taals)
  • Vibhag, Sam, Khali concepts
  • Layakari — tihais and dugun
🎼

Raag Vidya (Raag Theory)

  • 10 parent thaats (Melakarta system)
  • Aaroh, Avaroh, Pakad of each raag
  • Vadi & Samvadi swaras
  • Time and season of raags
  • Similar raag differentiation
🎤

Gayaki (Vocal Styles)

  • Dhrupad & Khayal gayaki
  • Thumri, Dadra, Bhajan
  • Bandish compositions
  • Alaap, Jod, Jhala development
  • Breath control and voice culture
📖

Sangeet Shastra (Music Theory)

  • History of Hindustani music
  • Gharana traditions
  • Famous compositions & composers
  • Notation (Bhatkhande swarlipi)
  • Rasa theory and aesthetics
🎹

Instrument Study

  • Harmonium — basic to advanced
  • Tabla — basic theka patterns
  • Tanpura drone practice
  • Sitar — introductory course
  • Bansuri (flute) — beginner track
